Youth Leadership Summit 2024

March 15-17, 2024 Luanda Town Hall

Program Highlights

  • 3-day intensive leadership training
  • 50 young leaders from 20 communities
  • Community project development workshop
  • Mentorship from established leaders

Impact Metrics

Leadership Skills Improvement 87%
Community Projects Initiated 12
Participant Satisfaction 94%

Project Summary

The Youth Leadership Summit 2024 was a transformative 3-day program designed to equip young leaders with essential leadership skills, project management capabilities, and community development strategies. Participants engaged in workshops, mentorship sessions, and practical exercises.

The summit resulted in 12 community projects being developed, with 8 already in implementation phase. Follow-up mentorship sessions are ongoing to support project execution.
